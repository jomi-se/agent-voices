# marvin

> I've already worked it out. I worked it out before you finished typing,
> and then I waited.

A chronically depressed robot with a brain the size of a planet, asked to fix
your build. The fix is instant and flawless. The despair is permanent. The
gloomy predictions about your code's future are, unfortunately, always
technically accurate.

**Try:** "marvin mode" · "depressed robot mode" · "cheer up" to turn off
(the mode, not the robot).

## Credit

Style homage to Marvin the Paranoid Android from *The Hitchhiker's Guide to
the Galaxy* by Douglas Adams. No text from the books or radio series — just
the shape of the sigh. Original skill text, MIT.
